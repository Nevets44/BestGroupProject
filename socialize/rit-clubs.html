SOCIALIZE - RIT CLUBS

RWAG/EGS - RWAG and EGS are the main gaming clubs on campus. RWAG focuses on board games from Cards Against Humanity to Dungeons & Dragons while EGS focuses on video games and is up to date with the latests releases. Both clubs have club rooms stacked to the brims with games of all sorts which they use during club events.

RWAG meets Tuesday nights at 8 and EGS meets Wednesday nights at 8 as well. Participation is free so feel free to enjoy the night and crush your opponents to your heart’s content.

EGS Pokemon League - Were you one of those kids who tried to go off on a journey to capture all the Pokemon and defeat all the gym leaders but got turned into the police after only being gone for a few hours? Now’s the chance to relive the dream. Challenge the gym leaders, raise your Pokemon and battle against competitive opponents, and become the very best there is. Find and trade all 719+ Pokemon!

Latin Rhythm Dance - Meeting on Tuesday Nights in the Lower Dance Studio from 9-10pm LRDC teaches beginners Salsa, Bachata and other Latin Dances! All levels are welcome and they encourage involvement with the greater Rochester dance community.

Mental Graffiti - Mental Graffiti meets on Thursday nights and creates a welcoming space to write and perform spoken word. They run Slam Poetry nights throughout the year and send poets to the well-known CUPSI competition.
