PAY TO PLAY

Altitude Trampoline Park - An indoor trampoline park located in Southtown Plaza. Some of the features include a basketball court, dodgeball area, foam pit, and obviously trampolines everywhere. If you get hungry you can grab some concession stand food like hotdogs, pretzels, nachos and chips or you can visit one of the food places located inside Southtown Plaza. There’s a bagel shop, and a huge Chinese buffet.

Pole Position Raceway - An indoor go-kart racetrack at Marketplace Mall. The go-karts are electric and can reach speeds up to 45 mph. Fun Fact: Racers are given different starting times depending on how fast they complete a lap in a preliminary lap. The winner of that lap gets “pole position” and gets to start ahead of the other racers.

Dave and Busters - Dave & Buster's is a national chain American restaurant and entertainment (mainly arcade games) business located in The Marketplace Mall, next to Pole Position Raceway. It is basically a Chuck E’ Cheese meant for adults! There’s a restaurant and bar with tons of options on the menu. There’s burgers, pasta, ribs, appetizers and desserts. We recommend you do the eat and play, where you can order a combo of food and gaming credits. It’s definitely worth it.

Laserquest - A huge laser tag facility on West Ridge Road in Greece, with a two-story laser tag room, a few arcade games, an air hockey and a foosball table, and a room to accommodate birthday parties. They give discounts to lock-ins and big group parties.

LASTERTRON - Lasertron is an adult laser tag facility in Henrietta, with a two separate laser tag rooms, two courts, a sports bar, a cafe and a few arcade games. What’s better than getting drunk and playing laser tag?! They have a lot of deals available for large groups, so if all your dorm floor mates or your organization wants to do some bonding, LASERTRON is the perfect place.
