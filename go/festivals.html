PLACES TO GO - FESTIVALS

Freezefest and Springfest - Two festivals that happen every year, the former is highlighted by a comedian where the latter is showcased by a popular band. Both events are full of a weekend of activities that bring students out around campus to have a fun break from the grind of class work.

ImagineRIT - A yearly tradition at RIT, this is a time for students from all majors to get together and produce projects to showcase their abilities. Families and the community come to the multiple day event to be wowed my projects that push technology and show what the future could look like through the eyes of students.

Fringefest - With over 500 performances and events each year this festival showcases theatre, comedy, visual arts, entertainment, music, poetry and more. The Fringe seeks to bring people together.

Balloon Festival - The New York Festival of Balloons invites spectators to watch hot air balloons take off and even go up in balloons themselves!
