PLACES TO GO - RECREATIONAL ACTIVITIES

Rochester Parkour - The Rochester Parkour Gym is Rochester's only parkour place. They have vault boxes, swing rings, climbing ropes, jungle gyms, bars, and climbing boxes. Classes are offered for kids and adults ages 3 to 65.

Hike - With many hiking and nature trails nearby the campus students will often take a short drive out to nature to enjoy a day of hiking and maybe a picnic. Ranging from easy walks down a path to more difficult hikes, adventurers of all experience levels can find something to enjoy.

Explore Rochester Subway Tunnels - Previously a Subway but then shut down, these tunnels have become a residence for the homeless and a canvas for graffiti. Many college students seek out this location to explore and photograph.
