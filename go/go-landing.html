Places to Go - Landing Page

Outside of places like theaters, shops, and other amenities that Rochester has to offer there is a huge variety of interesting and unique places for a young college student to visit and explore. Not being pushed by an activity they offer the opportunity to relax and experience the city at your own pace. This is a relaxing experience and some of these places are sites that cannot be missed. As freshman many RIT students spend hours exploring the city on weekends, some head a little further out to take in the fresh air and beauty of nature. Regardless of whether you find beauty in architecture or nature, there will be an experience and a nice view waiting for you in the Rochester area.
