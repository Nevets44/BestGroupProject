PLACES TO EAT

As college students, we are always looking for places to eat. Rochester has a huge variety of places to eat, there is something for everyone. There are the fast food options, the elegant ethnic cuisine, the popular bars, the late night eats and of course everyone’s go-to - the 24 hour places! You can ride solo or dine with a group of friends, we guarantee you that there’s a perfect place for you to become a regular.
