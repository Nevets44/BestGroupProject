ENTERTAINMENT - CONCERTS

Water Street Music Hall - The Water Street Music Hall is one of Rochester's largest places for live music. The place can hold about 800 people. Many famous people like Maroon 5 have played here.

Rochester Main Street Armory - This beautiful and historic building serves as a venue for a variety of things like concerts, performing arts, sports teams, and other events. It has facilities for serving food and drink.

The Bug Jar - Small bar setting that attracts acoustic, alternative and folk bands for intimate concert settings.
