ENTERTAINMENT - RIT CAMPUS EVENTS

Red Barn Climbing - The Red Barn is a barn on the RIT campus that has been converted into an indoor rock climbing and bouldering facility.

Human vs Zombies (HVZ) - Humans vs Zombies is a campus-wide game played twice a year that pits humans armed with NERF guns and zombies who chase and infect the humans. Battles can begin at anytime outside the buildings, so you can enjoy the sense of danger and running for your life as a human, or bring more over to the fold as you stalk your prey as a zombie.

No Voice Zone - No Voice Zone is a student-run organization that meets on Wednesday nights 10pm-11pm in the Student Development Center. Come and learn about Deaf Culture and ASL. This program, created by Res Life, looks the bridge the gap between hearing and deaf students in the RIT/NTID community.

Gray Matter - This monthly discussion forum covers topics that range from social awkwardness to racism and politics. The moderators of these events seek to create a safe space for people to ask questions and share opinions. Typically held in the MOSAIC Center, follow Gray Matters on facebook to see when the next forum will be!
